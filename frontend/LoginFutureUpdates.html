Our Future Features

Photo Detection

We’ve noticed that some users upload photos where their faces aren’t visible. To enhance authenticity, we’re integrating machine learning to ensure the first image you upload clearly shows your face—making connections more genuine from the start.

Quote Recommendations

Personalize your profile with ease. Using machine learning, we’ll recommend quotes tailored to your preferences. Simply choose the ones you love, and they’ll be added to your account to reflect your unique personality.

GIF Uploads

Express yourself dynamically! Soon, you’ll be able to upload GIFs just like quotes. These will appear on your profile, adding a fun and lively touch to how you present yourself to the Amourette community.

See How Much You Match with Trending Profiles

Discover how closely you align with today’s trending profiles! Using the details you provided during account creation, we’ll calculate a personalized compatibility score that shows how well you match with the most popular profiles currently making waves.
