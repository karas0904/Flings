Cookie Policy

At Amourette, we use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand how you interact with our site, personalize content, and improve performance.

We use essential cookies to make sure basic functions like logging in and navigating the site work smoothly. Without these, some features might not be available. We also use analytics cookies to track usage patterns—like which pages you visit most—so we can make Amourette better over time.

This helps us tailor recommendations and show relevant profiles. Could limit your exprience if not for cookies.

We don’t use cookies to collect sensitive personal information without your consent. For questions, feel free to contact us at support@amourette.com.
