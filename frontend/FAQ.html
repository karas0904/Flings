Maybe Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To join Amourette, visit our loginpage and click "Sign Up." You’ll need to provide a SRM email address, and fill out a few details about yourself.

How do I report someone?

If something feels off, go to the messaging page and select "Report," in three dot icon dropdown or email us at support@amourette.com. Provide specifics, and we’ll handle it.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account anytime through your Account page. Navigate to "Account," select "Delete Account," and confirm. Your data will be removed in line with our privacy standards.

What if I encounter a technical issue?

We’re here to help! Report any glitches via the "Report a Problem" page. Include a description of the issue, and we’ll resolve it as quickly as possible.

What Are Trending Pages?

The Trending page showcases the top 10 most popular profiles each week, ranked by the number of likes they receive. It’s a great way to discover who’s catching attention in the Amourette community!

Why Can’t I Comment?

To leave a comment, you’ll need to send a rose to that person first. Once the rose is sent, the comment box will unlock, letting you share your thoughts.

Where Can I See Who Liked My Profile?

Head to your Account page, then click the bell icon. A dropdown will appear, showing everyone who has liked your profile—all in one convenient spot.

What Is Buddy Up and Efforts For?

Buddy Up is designed to connect you with others for study sessions via video call, once both sides agree. In the Efforts section, you can see who has commented on or sent roses to you.

Why Can I See My Old Likes and Saves?

Saves are visible for one week before they expire. Likes from others remain viewable for five days, giving you a short window to see who’s shown interest in your profile.
