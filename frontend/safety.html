Your Safety Matters

Guided by Our Core Beliefs

At Amourette, we live by Authenticity, Courage, and Empathy—our ACE principles. These values shape our community, guiding how we connect both online and in person.

Constant Protection

Our safety team blends smart technology with human oversight to keep Amourette a secure space. Reports are handled swiftly—whether by automated systems or our expert moderators—to ensure your peace of mind.

Easy Reporting

Feel something’s off? Report it anytime. Once flagged, that person disappears from your view, and your report stays confidential to keep you safe.

Date Follow-Up

We check in after your dates with a quick survey. Loved the vibe? Great. Not so much? Let us know privately if anything felt wrong—it helps us maintain a respectful community.

Kindness Required

Respect is non-negotiable. Rudeness, threats, or misuse of our tools (like fake reports) isn’t tolerated. We’re here for real connections, not drama.
